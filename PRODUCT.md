# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Systemio staff who review incoming website enquiries, qualify prospects, move
leads through the sales workflow, and prepare follow-up messages. The dashboard
is an internal daily-use tool, usually operated by one person but designed to
remain clear when more staff are added.

## Product Purpose

Give the team one dependable place to triage new enquiries, understand customer
context quickly, organize leads by stage, and prepare a response without losing
information between tools. Success means a new lead can be understood and moved
forward in under a minute.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, technical. The product should feel trustworthy and quietly
capable, with plain language and confident restraint.

## Anti-references

Avoid flashy SaaS dashboards, decorative glass effects, dense enterprise
control panels, novelty navigation, gratuitous metrics, and interfaces that
look generated from a generic component gallery.

## Design Principles

1. Put the next operational decision ahead of decorative reporting.
2. Keep customer context visible while actions remain close at hand.
3. Use familiar controls consistently so the interface disappears into the task.
4. Explain empty, loading, and failure states with a clear next step.
5. Preserve Systemio's dark, precise identity without sacrificing readability.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and keyboard-navigation expectations. Respect reduced
motion, provide visible focus states, avoid color-only status communication,
and keep layouts usable from mobile widths through large desktop screens.
